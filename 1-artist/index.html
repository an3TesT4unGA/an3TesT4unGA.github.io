STORMY PYEATTE

INDEPENDENT ARTIST

Stormy Pyeatte is an independent artist, photographer, videographer and social media content creator living in London, UK. Both left and (mostly) right-brained, Stormy blends her techy-know-how and sense of imagination to craft a wide variety of social media content for brands. She has five years of experience working as a content creator both in-house and as a freelancer.
